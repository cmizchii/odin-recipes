Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy, known for its crispy, chewy crust topped with a flavorful tomato sauce, melted cheese, and an array of delicious toppings. The versatility of pizza allows for endless variations, whether it's a classic Margherita with fresh basil, a meat lover's pizza loaded with sausage and pepperoni, or a veggie-packed version with mushrooms, peppers, and onions. The dough is typically made from flour, yeast, water, and olive oil, then baked at high temperatures to create a golden, bubbly, and slightly charred crust. Pizza is a perfect balance of savory, cheesy goodness, and it's enjoyed worldwide as a comfort food, perfect for sharing with friends or family at any time of day.

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

for the sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

for the toppings:

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Additional toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.

  2. Add olive oil, salt, and flour. Mix until a dough forms. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.

  3. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.

  4. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté minced garlic for 1-2 minutes. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.

  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Preheat a pizza stone if using.

  6. Punch down dough, divide into two portions (or keep whole), and roll out to desired thickness.

  7. Place dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread sauce, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and add toppings.

  8. Unwrap, sprinkle with flour, and deflate gently. Roll to a 14x6-inch rectangle, and fold again. Turn 90 degrees, and repeat. Wrap and chill 2 hours.

  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.

  10. Remove from oven, add fresh basil, slice, and serve.
